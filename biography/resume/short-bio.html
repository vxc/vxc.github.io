Venkatesh Choppella Short Biography

Venkatesh Choppella is Associate Professor at the Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management, Kerala (IIITM-K). He is also Adjunct Faculty at the Corporate Learning Centre at Tata Consultancy Services. He holds a Master's and PhD. degree in Computer Science from Indiana University and a Bachelor's degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur. Before arriving at IIITM-K in 2003, he held research and engineering positions in Hewlett Packard, Xerox Palo Alto Research Centre, Oak Ridge National Laboratory in the United States.

Prof. Choppella's research interests are in programming language and software systems. He has published in the area of automated deduction, type systems, formal methods, and compilers for high performance computing. Two of the papers he co-authored recently received the best paper awards at the Internation Conf. of High Performance Computing (HiPC) 2003 and The International Parallel and Distributed Processing Symposium (IPDPS) 2004.

At IIITM-K he has also been coordinating the development of web-based collaborative systems for e-governance (See the portal www.tvmcitypolice.org).
